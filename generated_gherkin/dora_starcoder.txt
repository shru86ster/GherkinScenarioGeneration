For the following requirement, generate Gherkin scenarios: The application should fetch live market prices with a latency of less than 1 second.
Feature: Live market price fetch
In order to track the market prices more effectively and accurately
As a user
I want to fetch the live market prices

Scenario: Fetch live market prices
Given a user is on the market prices page
When the user requests a live market price
Then the system should fetch the live market prices
And the system should respond with the live market prices within 1 second

Scenario: Fetch live market prices in case of network outage
Given a user is on the market prices page
When the user requests a live market price
And the user's network connection is interrupted
Then the system should fetch the live market prices
But the system should respond with the live market prices within 1 second
