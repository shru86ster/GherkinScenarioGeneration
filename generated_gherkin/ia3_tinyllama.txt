For the following requirement, generate Gherkin scenarios: The application should fetch live market prices with a latency of less than 1 second.
As a user, I want to fetch live market prices, so that I can make informed investment decisions.

Feature: Market Fetching
  Scenario: Fetch live market prices
    Given system has the requirement to fetch live market prices
    When market data fetching module is invoked
    Then market data is fetched with a latency of less than 1 second

  Scenario: Fetch market prices with an invalid URL
    Given system has an invalid URL to fetch live market prices
    When market data fetching module is invoked
    Then an error is thrown with a message "Invalid URL"
    And the market data fetching module is not invoked
